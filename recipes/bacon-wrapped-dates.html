Bacon Wrapped Dates

Description:

Dates are stuffed with cream cheese, wrapped in bacon and baked until crisp. These are delicious and very easy to make for a party. You can serve them at room temperature, so it is okay to make a few hours in advance!

Ingredients:

  • toothpicks (soaked in water for at least 30 minutes)
  • 1 pound sliced bacon, cut in half
  • 1 pound pitted dates, sliced halfway lengthwise
  • 4 oz cream cheese

Steps:

  1. Preheat oven to 375 degrees F (190 degrees C)
  2. Spread cream cheese into the center of the dates. Close the halves of the dates, and wrap a half-slice of bacon around the outside. Secure each one with a toothpick. Arrange in a baking dish or on a baking sheet with sides to catch any grease.
  3. Bake for 30-40 minutes in the preheated oven, or until the bacon is crisp. Turn dates over after the first 20 minutes for even cooking.
