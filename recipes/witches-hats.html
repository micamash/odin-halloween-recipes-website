Witches' Hats

Description:

Witches' hats are cute and simple Halloween treats that everyone will love. The amount this recipe makes depends on how many cookies and chocolate kisses you have.

Ingredients:

  • 2 (16 oz) packages fudge stripe cookies
  • 1/4 cup honey, or as needed
  • 1 (9 oz) bag milk chocolate candy kisses, unwrapped
  • 1 (4.5 oz) tube decorating gel

Steps:

  1. Witches' hats are cute and simple Halloween treats that everyone will love. The amount this recipe makes depends on how many cookies and chocolate kisses you have.
  2. Use decorating gel to pipe a small bow onto the cookie at the base of the candy piece. Repeat with remaining ingredients.
